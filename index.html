Wayfinding

Your Route to the Lab

1 — Locate Dauterive Hall on Campus

Head to 635 Downey Way on USC's University Park Campus. VPD sits between Trousdale Parkway to the west and Pardee Way to the east. The south entrance faces USC Gould School of Law; the north entrance faces USC Price School of Public Policy and Childs Way. Exposition Blvd runs along the south edge of campus nearby.

🅿️ Nearest parking: Parking Structure X (via Pardee Way entrance)
Google Maps USC Interactive Map

2 — Enter the Building

Walk into Dauterive Hall through either entrance — the south entrance (facing the Law School) or the north entrance (facing Price School / Childs Way). You'll be on the first floor. The lab is below you on the Lower Level.

3 — Take Stairs or Elevator Down

Head to the central staircase or the elevator and go down one level to the Lower Level (LL). When you exit, you'll be in the lower level common area.

♿ Elevator available for accessibility

4 — Find Your Assigned Suite

The lab has three suites along the lower level. Look for the door signs and check in with the research assistant. Your SONA confirmation email tells you which suite to go to.

BUAD 307 (Marketing) → LL-102 Computer Lab
BUAD 304 / 497 (MOR) → LL-112 or LL-110 Suite
